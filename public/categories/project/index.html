eink calendar: Part 1

When I first started using org-mode on Emacs, the agenda option seemed like too much hassle. There was too much programming involved, too many shortcuts to remember (like anything Emacs), and it just wasn’t pretty. But mid-2019, most other methods to become organized just didn’t click, and org-agenda seemed ever-more appealing. With a little bit of effort (or in my case, copy-paste) in the configuration, I’ve managed to get something that works.
